479 Stuy Fusion
We are Stuy Fusion, a part of Stuyvesant High School's Robotics organization devoted to building and programming robots for the FTC competition. Over the course of the school year, we construct and program an innovative robot to compete and later demo with, while raising funds and spreading the ideals of FIRST within and beyond our community.
POWERPLAY℠
POWERPLAY℠ is this year's FTC game. Our team follows the industry standard model of design thinking, which consists of brainstorming, prototyping, and refining. This process allows us to better understand the mechanisms we build, while possessing the flexibity of an open minded mindset and room for improvement.
